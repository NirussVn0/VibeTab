# Privacy Policy for VibeTab

**Last Updated:** December 10, 2025

## 1. Data Collection
**VibeTab does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

*   **Offline First:** All data (widgets, settings, layouts) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's `storage.local` and `IndexedDB` APIs.
*   **No Analytics:** We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party tracking scripts.
*   **No Servers:** VibeTab has no backend servers. Your data never leaves your browser.

## 2. External Services
VibeTab may interact with the following third-party services only upon your direct action:
*   **Search Providers:** If you use the Search Widget, queries are sent directly to your chosen provider (Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo) via their standard public URLs.
*   **Weather API (Optional):** If you enable the Weather widget, your generic location (City name) is sent to the OpenWeatherMap API to fetch current conditions.

## 3. Permissions
VibeTab requests the following permissions for functionality only:
*   `storage`: To save your grid layout and settings.
*   `bookmarks` / `topSites`: To display your bookmarks and most visited sites in widgets (if enabled).

## 4. Contact
For questions or support, please open an issue on our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/sabiofvibelab/vibetab).
